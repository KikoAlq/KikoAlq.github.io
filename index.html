SUNY Potsdam Campus Life Fall 2017 - Spring 2018

I joined Campus Life as a Graphic Designer in 2017, and while attending classes, I designed posters, banners, brocures, and graphics for mailers, social media posts, e-mail campaigns, and various print materials for weekly/seasonal events. Until and through the Spring 2018 semester, I remained at my position at Campus Life, while also taking up an Internship at the Office of College Communications at SUNY Potsdam, and collaborated on a number of events around the campus, including the Crane school of Music.

Vegas Strong 2017

Vegas Night is a event that is hosted yearly in Potsdam, but this event was one that was almost cancelled at the last minute. That year, a mass shooting occurred when 64-year-old Stephen Paddock opened fire on the crowd attending the Route 91 Harvest music festival on the Las Vegas Strip in Nevada from his 32nd-floor suites in the Mandalay Bay hotel. He fired more than 1,000 rounds, killing 60 people and wounding at least 413. ("2017 Las Vegas Shooting", n.d.) Instead of fully cancelling the event, the leaders at Campus Life decided to switch the focus of the branding and begin a fundraiser for a fund that supported families and victims of the shooting. The design work from the first two weeks were scrapped, and within the next week I needed to fully redo what was needed for the event to reflect a drastic change in tone.

LOKO Arts Festival Snapchat Filters

I designed snapchat filters, with adherence to the design standards of the LOKO Arts festival. The Loko Arts Festival was started in 2012, with the support of Kathy (Kofoed) Lougheed, and her husband Don Lougheed, whose faces feature on the filter. During the festival, SUNY Potsdam celebrates the campus culture of creativity and reinforces how important the arts are to learning environments for every discipline as well as a lifetime of creativity for anyone who is crazy for the arts. (from potsdam.edu/about/campus/creative/loko/about)
